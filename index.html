Key Project Pillars

Project Rationale: The Why

The Oman Medical Specialty Board (OMSB) stands at a critical juncture in its digital evolution. Core processes and data remain fragmented across siloed systems, despite successful pilots of initiatives like the MASAR e-portfolio and AR/VR trials.

Strategy Mandate: Modernize operations, achieve alignment with the national digital agenda, and secure regional leadership in medical education by 2036.

The strategy directly supports Oman Vision 2040’s emphasis on excellent governance and human capital development.

Target State: The What

By 2035, OMSB will operate as a seamless, data-driven, and user-centric ecosystem unifying all core medical education functions (e.g., e-assessment, credentialing, CPD) under a single secure cloud platform.

Transformation Outcomes:

  • ✓Real-Time Engagement: Stakeholders receive personalized insights and trusted credentials.
  • ✓Operational Impact: Enables faster, paperless services and improved data-driven decision-making.
